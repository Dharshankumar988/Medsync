# User Guide

## For Patients
- **Dashboard**: View your upcoming appointments and recently uploaded medical files.
- **AI Chat**: Ask questions about your symptoms or medical history. The AI retrieves your secure records to provide contextual answers.
- **Blockchain Verification**: You can verify that your medical files have not been tampered with by clicking the "Verify" badge next to any record.

## For Doctors
- **Patients Queue**: View patients scheduled for the day.
- **Upload & Analyze**: Upload patient X-rays or MRIs. The built-in Computer Vision pipeline will highlight potential anomalies automatically.
- **Prescriptions**: Write digital prescriptions that are immediately pushed to the Blockchain and made available to registered pharmacies.

## For Pharmacies
- **Order Fulfillment**: When a patient arrives, scan their Prescription QR code (or search their ID). The system will cryptographically verify the prescription against the blockchain before allowing you to dispense the medication.
